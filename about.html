About Us


Castle History


Pembrook Castle, a magnificent fortress located in the breathtaking landscapes of Scotland, has a rich and storied history spanning centuries. Originally built in the 12th century, it served as a strategic stronghold during various conflicts, bearing witness to the shifting tides of Scottish history.


Over time, Pembrook Castle has transformed from a formidable medieval fortress to a celebrated cultural landmark, captivating visitors with its captivating architecture and fascinating tales of the past.

Castle Grounds


Nestled within the grounds of Pembrook Castle, the historical gardens have enchanted visitors for centuries with their breathtaking beauty and horticultural splendor. Designed in the 18th century by renowned landscape architects, the gardens boast meticulously manicured lawns, vibrant flower beds, and majestic ancient trees that create a tranquil oasis amidst the castle's rugged surroundings. As you wander through the garden's winding pathways, you'll discover charming fountains, ornate statues, and hidden nooks, each telling a unique story of the castle's past.


These meticulously maintained gardens served as a source of inspiration for poets, painters, and nature enthusiasts alike, evoking a sense of serenity and awe.
